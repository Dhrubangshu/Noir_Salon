About Us

Welcome to Noir Salon, your premier destination for exceptional hair and beauty services in the vibrant city of Guwahati. As the leading salon in the region, we take pride in offering a personalized and luxurious experience to each of our valued clients. At Noir Salon, our team of skilled stylists is committed to delivering top-notch services that go beyond the ordinary. We specialize in precision cuts, vibrant coloring, and the latest trends in hairstyles, ensuring that you leave our salon with a look that perfectly complements your individual style. Located in the heart of Guwahati, our modern and chic salon provides a welcoming and comfortable environment where sophistication meets relaxation. Our commitment to excellence extends to understanding and prioritizing your unique preferences. We believe that each client is unique, and our stylists take the time to listen and tailor their services to meet your specific style and beauty needs. Whether you're seeking a bold transformation or a subtle enhancement, Noir Salon is dedicated to making it happen.
